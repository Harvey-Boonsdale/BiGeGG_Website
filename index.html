BiGeGG is music I make in my attic in Sheffield.

Mainly guitar-based but with an electronic element throughout, I am mainly influenced by alternative, post punk, post rock and electronic music.

I have been making music as BiGeGG since 2020 - after playing bass and guitar in bands most of my life, I wanted to do something on my own bringing together my various influences.

I write, produce and record all my music myself and play all instruments with occasional guest spots from friends. I can't and don't sing.
